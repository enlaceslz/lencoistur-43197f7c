description: Metodologia de landing pages orientada a conversão: um objetivo primário por página, entrega above-the-fold (título / subtítulo / visual / CTA / prova social), fluxo de copy PAS (Problema-Agitação-Solução) e disciplina de A/B testing. Define os tipos de página por objetivo (captação, venda, evento, produto) e entrega estrutura e copy prontos.

Analyze and improve the landing page(s) of this project for maximum conversion.

1. ONE GOAL AUDIT — Does each landing page have exactly one primary conversion goal? Multiple competing CTAs (Sign Up + Learn More + Watch Demo + Contact) reduce conversion rates. Identify and remove all secondary CTAs or demote them visually.

2. ABOVE-THE-FOLD — Within the first screenful (without scrolling): Is the headline benefit-focused (what the user gets, not what the product does)? Is there a single, clear CTA button? Is there supporting credibility (number of users, logos, ratings)? Can a new visitor understand what the product is in under 5 seconds?

3. SOCIAL PROOF — Where is social proof placed? Best placements: immediately after the hero, adjacent to the CTA button, and in a dedicated testimonials section. Are the testimonials specific and credible (real name, photo, company, specific result)?

4. CTA DESIGN — Primary CTA: high contrast color (not the same as secondary elements), action-verb + benefit ("Start Free Trial", "Get My Free Report", not "Submit"), adequate size (min 200px wide, 50px tall), positioned above the fold and at least once more after each value section.

5. PAGE STRUCTURE — Does the page follow a proven conversion sequence? Recommended: Problem (header) → Solution (hero) → How it works (features) → Social proof → Pricing or secondary CTA → FAQ → Final CTA → Footer. Identify any missing sections.

6. FRICTION POINTS — What prevents a visitor from converting? Check: form fields (each additional field reduces conversion ~10%), required account creation before showing value, unclear pricing, no trust signals near the CTA, slow page load.

Provide a prioritized list of 5 specific changes, ordered by expected conversion impact.
